FACTION PROFILE: The Daughters of the Faithful

A decentralized, matriarchal sect with roots in ancient Ashkenazi Jewish mysticism and eschatology. The "Daughters" are not a conventional organization but a bloodline of "Witnesses" who have passed down a forbidden knowledge: that our reality is a fragile membrane, and its eventual rupture is not a prophecy to be feared, but a historical inevitability to be prepared for.

Their core belief is that history is cyclical, punctuated by these violent "Collisions." They do not worship a deity in a traditional sense; they "keep faith" with the immutable truth of the cycle. They are the keepers of memory, the chroniclers of apocalypses past and future. While other factions build weapons or seek power, the Daughters weave narratives, preserve forbidden histories, and maintain safe-houses in locations they have identified as future impact zones.

This makes them invaluable, and incredibly dangerous, to all other factions now scrambling to comprehend the new reality.
